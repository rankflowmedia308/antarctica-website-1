Cookie Policy & Privacy Policy

How this site handles your data — plainly explained.

Last updated: March 2026

About This Site

Best Antarctica Cruise Companies (best-antarctica-cruise-companies.com) is an independent editorial website. We do not sell products, process payments, or create user accounts. The information below describes our data practices in plain language.

What Data We Collect

We do not collect personal information directly. If basic analytics (such as Google Analytics) are enabled, aggregated, anonymized data about page visits may be recorded — including approximate geographic region, device type, and pages viewed. This data does not identify you personally and is used solely to understand which content is useful to readers.

We do not collect names, email addresses, payment details, or any personally identifying information through normal use of this website.

Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser. This site may use:

  • Analytics cookies (if Google Analytics is active): Used to measure anonymous traffic patterns. These do not identify you personally.
  • Functional cookies: If present, these remember basic preferences such as navigation state. They do not track you across other websites.

You can disable cookies at any time through your browser settings. Disabling cookies will not affect your ability to read any content on this site — all content is fully accessible without cookies or JavaScript.

Third-Party Links

This site contains links to expedition cruise operator websites. When you click a link and visit an external site, that site's own privacy policy and cookie practices apply. We have no control over third-party data practices and recommend reviewing their policies before submitting any personal or payment information.

All outbound links on this site are direct editorial links. We do not use affiliate tracking links or earn commission from referrals.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Anonymized, aggregated analytics data may be processed by analytics service providers (e.g., Google Analytics) under their respective terms of service.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (including EEA/GDPR, UK GDPR, and CCPA), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete data held about you, and to opt out of analytics tracking. Since we collect no personal data directly, most of these rights are satisfied by the absence of personal data collection. For analytics opt-out, visit Google Analytics Opt-out or use your browser's built-in privacy tools.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at contact@best-antarctica-cruise-companies.com.
