About Us

Independent rankings and expert reviews of Antarctica expedition cruise operators.

Who We Are

Best Antarctica Cruise Companies is an independent editorial resource run by a small team of travel researchers with a deep focus on polar expedition cruising. Our collective background spans years of field research, operator evaluations, traveler interviews, and direct experience with Antarctic and subantarctic voyages. We are not affiliated with any cruise line, travel agency, or booking platform.

Our Mission

Antarctica attracts a growing number of travelers who want to experience one of the world's last genuinely wild places. The challenge is that choosing the right expedition operator — from among dozens of IAATO-certified companies with similar-looking itineraries — is genuinely complex. Ship size, time ashore, expedition team quality, activity range, and price tier all interact in ways that marketing materials rarely clarify honestly.

Our mission is simple: give travelers the research-based, operator-neutral information they need to make a confident booking decision. We rank operators on documented criteria, update our assessments regularly, and never accept payment for placement or editorial coverage.

How We Work

Our rankings are based on publicly verifiable criteria: IAATO membership status, vessel specifications (capacity, ice class, hull type), declared activities and optional add-ons, guest-to-guide ratios where disclosed, traveler feedback aggregated from multiple independent review platforms, and significant operational developments such as fleet additions or itinerary changes. We do not rank operators that are not active IAATO members. Rankings are reviewed annually, with interim updates when operators make significant changes to their fleet or programs.

Editorial Independence

No operator, agency, or third party has paid for placement, inclusion, or favorable coverage on this site. All outbound links to operator websites are direct editorial links — not affiliate or tracking links. We do not earn commission on bookings. If you believe any information on this site is inaccurate, please contact us and we will investigate and correct it promptly.
